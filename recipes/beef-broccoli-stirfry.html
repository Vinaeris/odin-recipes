Beef & Broccoli Stir Fry

Description

A delicious, easy beef and broccoli stir-fry recipe for a wholesome meal.

Great with some fresh-steamed rice and easily a family favourite.

Ingredients

Beef & broccoli ingredients

  • 450g beef steak, sliced into bite-sized strips
  • 450g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 fresh chillies, sliced thinly
  • olive oil

Stir-fry sauce ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated (or 1 tsp ginger powder)
  • 6 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • pepper to taste
  • paprike to taste
  • cayenne to taste
  • water as preferred

Steps

  1. Prep: Start cooking white rice first so it's ready when the stir fry is done. Cover and freeze steak for 30 minutes for easy slicing.
  2. Combine all stir-fry sauce ingredients in a bowl and stir well to dissolve. Set aside.
  3. Place a large skillet over medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Add broccoli florets and sautee for 4-5 minutes, stirring or tossing until broccoli is bright-green and crisp-tender. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. Increase heat to high and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Add beef in single layer and sautee until just cooked through, about 2 minutes per side. If necessary, remove and repeat with extra beef slices.
  5. Once all beef slices are cooked through, reduce heat to low. Refill pan with beef (if applicable) and add sauce, mixing it with the beef. Let simmer for 3 to 4 minutes.
  6. Add broccoli and stir to combine. Add about 1/4 cup water to thin sauce, if desired. Gently simmer for about 2 minutes
  7. Serve over white rice!
